Why we built Axum Scholars

We started Axum Scholars because many students have the talent to win scholarships, but don't always have a clear map for the process. Requirements can feel confusing, and reliable information can be scattered across many websites.

Our goal is simple: make the scholarship journey easier to understand, easier to follow, and easier to finish with confidence.
