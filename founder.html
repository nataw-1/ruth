Founder

We are a group of high school students who created Axum Scholars to make international scholarship information easier to find. On our projects, we learned that students often struggle to locate clear steps, reliable requirements, and trustworthy guidance. That is why we are building a platform that helps students understand the process and prepares them to apply with confidence. In short: we want to support student-led dreams and turn scholarship opportunities into something more accessible.

Axum Scholars

A platform created by high school students to help other students discover and access international scholarship opportunities.
