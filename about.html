About Us

Axum Scholars is designed primarily for junior and senior high school students around the world. Our aim is to support high school students by guiding them through the steps required to apply for scholarships in countries such as the United States.

What makes us special?

The website has a unique feature compared to many other websites: its courses are available in both Amharic and English versions, making the information more accessible to a wider group of students.

Quick Highlight

Bilingual courses (Amharic & English) to broaden access for students.
