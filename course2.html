Course II

Step 1 - 4

  1. Research colleges & programs (የዩኒቨርሲቲ እና የትምህርት ምርጫ)
    • Search for universities in the USA (በአሜሪካ ያሉ ዩኒቨርሲቲዎችን መፈለግ)
    • Choose your major (የሚማሩትን የትምህርት ዘርፍ መምረጥ)
    • Compare tuition fees, location and rankings (ክፍያ፣ ቦታ እና ደረጃ ማነጻጸር)
  2. Understand requirements (መስፈርቶችን መረዳት)
    • High school transcript (የሁለተኛ ደረጃ ውጤት)
    • English test (IELTS / TOEFL / Duolingo) (የእንግሊዘኛ ቋንቋ ብቃት ፈተና)
    • Recommendation letters (የድጋፍ ደብዳቤ)
    • Personal statement (የራስ መግለጫ)
  3. Prepare your documents (ሰነዶችን ማዘጋጀት)
    • Translate documents into English (ሰነዶችን ወደ እንግሊዘኛ መተርጎም)
    • Prepare certificates and transcripts (ሰርተፊኬቶችና ውጤቶች ማዘጋጀት)
    • Write your personal statement (የራስ መግለጫ መጻፍ)
  4. Apply to universities (ለዩኒቨርሲቲዎች ማመልከት)
    • Apply through university websites or Common App (በዩኒቨርሲቲ ድረ-ገጾች ወይም በኮመን አፕ ማመልከት)
    • Upload all documents (ሁሉንም ሰነዶች መጫን)
    • Pay application fee if required (የማመልከቻ ክፍያ መክፈል)
    • Submit before deadline (ከቀነ ገደቡ በፊት ማስገባት)

Required docs

  • High school transcripts
  • Test scores (if required)
  • Recommendation letters
  • Personal statement / essays
  • Passport details

Submit checklist

  • Check spelling + contact info
  • Verify program name + major
  • Submit before the last day
