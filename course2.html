Course II

Step 1 - 6

  1. Research colleges & programs Search for universities in the US. Choose your major (program), compare tuition/fees, and check entry requirements.
  2. Understand requirements Review tests (if needed), transcripts, language rules, and deadlines for each program.
  3. Prepare documents Gather transcripts, recommendation letters, test results, and your personal statement.
  4. Fill the application Complete forms carefully and double-check everything before submitting.
  5. Pay application fee (if required) Confirm payment method and keep proof of submission.
  6. Submit before deadline Submit early, and save copies of what you uploaded.

Required docs

  • High school transcripts
  • Test scores (if required)
  • Recommendation letters
  • Personal statement / essays
  • Passport details

Submit checklist

  • Check spelling + contact info
  • Verify program name + major
  • Submit before the last day
