Welcome to Axum Scholars

Welcome to Axum Scholars. A platform created by high school students to help other students discover and access international scholarship opportunities and simplify the application processes for countries such as the United States, United Kingdom, and Italy.

Our mission is to provide clear and reliable information about international scholarship opportunities and scholarship application processes for countries such as the United States, United Kingdom, and Italy.
