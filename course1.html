Key terms

International students
An individual who travels from their home country to another country for the purpose of studying.
Visa / Permit
A document issued by a country that grants permission for an entry or acceptance into that country for a specified period and purpose.
Admission / Acceptance
The process by which individuals are granted college or university admission/acceptance after meeting requirements.

The best time you can begin your college journey is the summer before your senior year, so you can prepare early for fall and spring semesters.

Fall Semester

  • Start in August / September
  • Ends in December

Spring Semester

  • Start in January
  • Ends in May

Summer Semester

  • Start in May / June
  • Ends in July / August
